Welcome to spawn town! It is intended to help new players get started and to act as a life line for those who have died and need a bit of help getting back to their base. If you're not sure what to do or you need help, feel free to post to the forum thread!

Spawn Town

Various supplies are available here to help you get started. While you are free to take any items you find (some chests are locked to store spawn building supplies) it is best that you take just what you need and leave the rest for players to come!

Different buildings store different supplies. For example, you can find free iron at the blacksmith and gourmet meals at the cafe.

  • Gulu Gulu Cafe and Hippo Bakery
  • Inside are chests full of delicious meals!

The Graveyard

Of special interest in spawn town is a graveyard in the north east corner of town.

Players may use the "services" of the crypt to have their head removed. After that, you may place your head on any gravestone. You can only place/remove your own head, so don't worry about anyone running off with it.

Finding Your New Home

Since this was one of the first SMP servers, there is a fair bit of craziness around spawn. There are lots of old bases and abandoned construction projects from when the server had 20-30 people online at once.

Using the server's world map you can search for an unoccupied location. The "Night" mode (in the upper left) as well as the marker and rail overlays can help you determine if someone is already in a location.

A fairly large rail network has been build in the nether to help players travel to remote locations. This rail network is represented on the world map using pink/grey rail icons as well as red lines. The grey icons are the main rail stations at nether spawn. The pink icons represent sub stations that have portals to the overworld. The red lines represent the path the rail line takes in the nether. Use some iron from the blacksmith if you do not yet have a minecart.

So how do you get to the nether? That's entirely up to you to figured out. On a complete unrelated note, there are rumors of men in black robes going into the basement of the tavern...
